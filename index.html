How to Play Color Tiles

Master the color tiles game in four simple steps:

  1. Tap Blank Spaces: Click on any blank space on the game board.
  2. Match Colors: Collect neighboring tiles (vertically or horizontally) that match the color of the space you tapped. You need at least 2 tiles of the same color to collect them.
  3. Score Points: Earn 1 point for each tile collected.
  4. Beat the Clock: Score as many points as possible within the 120-second time limit!

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Click on groups of 2 or more same-colored tiles that are adjacent (horizontally or vertically) to a tapped blank space.
  • Cleared tiles become blank spaces.
  • Plan your moves to create chain reactions and maximize your score.
