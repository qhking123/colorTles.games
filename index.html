💡 Pro Tip: Long-Distance Connection

As long as the path is clear (no other colored tiles), you can connect matching tiles across any number of blank spaces. This is the key to clearing the entire board!

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Click on blank spaces to trigger a cross-scan in 4 directions.
  • If 2 or more nearest tiles have the same color, they are removed.
  • Clear the board strategically to reach the target score before time runs out!
  • Advance through 12 challenging levels with increasing complexity.

What is Google Color Tiles Game?

Google Color Tiles (also known as Google Colour Tiles) is an addictive puzzle game where you tap blank spaces to trigger a cross-scan for matching colors. Challenge yourself with this classic colour tiles Google game featuring 12 levels and a progressive difficulty system!

Strategic Gameplay

Tap on blank spaces to scan Up, Down, Left, and Right. If the first tile in two or more directions matches, they clear!

12-Level System

From the "Easy Start" to the "True Hell" of Level 12, each level reduces the time limit and adds more colors to the board.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you play the Google Colour Tiles game?
A: Playing the colour tiles google game is simple! Tap on any blank space (gray dashed boxes). The game scans Up, Down, Left, and Right for the nearest colored tiles. If 2 or more match, they clear! Reach the target score before the level timer runs out.
Q: What is the scoring system in Google Color Tiles?
A: You earn 1 point for each tile you collect. To advance, you must reach the target score for the current level (e.g., 100 points). The faster you clear, the better your chances of beating the tight timers in higher levels!
Q: Are there different difficulty levels?
A: Yes! Our version features 12 progressive levels. Level 1 starts with 3 colors and 60 seconds, while the legendary Level 12 features 8 colors and only a 12-second time limit!
Q: Can I play the color tiles game on mobile devices?
A: Absolutely! Our Google Color Tiles game is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The touch controls are optimized for mobile gameplay while maintaining the precision needed for strategic play.
Q: Is there a strategy to get higher scores in the color tiles game?
A: Yes! Look for areas with large clusters of the same color before tapping. Plan your moves to create chain reactions and always prioritize spaces that will give you the most matching tiles. Practice helps you recognize patterns faster.
Q: Can I compete with friends in Google Color Tiles game?
A: Yes! You can share your scores with friends and challenge them to beat your high score. The game includes social features that let you compare performance and compete on leaderboards with other color tiles game players.
